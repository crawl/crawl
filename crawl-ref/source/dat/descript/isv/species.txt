%%%%
Barachi

Barači sųt pomali zemnovodni humanoidi, ktori mogųt skakati na velike dalje.
Iz-za jih råzširjenogo vida jih takože možno lėgko uględěti s velikogo dalja.
%%%%
Coglin

Koglini nosęt začarovane ekzoskelety, ktore sposobne vlåděti orųžjem v oboh
rųkah, ovivajųći pomalo prěměšćajųći sę metal okolo svojih krěhkyh goblinoidnyh
těles.
%%%%
Deep Elf

Glųbinni Elfi imajųt vysokų naklonnost́ kȯ vsim sortam magije, ale oni sųt veĺmi
krěhki. Oni bystro regenerujųt magičnų silų.
%%%%
Demigod

Polubogi odkazyvajųt sę služiti vśakomu bogu, ale kompensujųt to božskymi atributami
i vysokymi rezervami zdråvja i magije.
%%%%
Demonspawn

Demonični Potomki sųt polu-ljudi, polu-demoni. S jih råstom oni nabyvajųt siĺne
demonične mutacije.
%%%%
Djinni

Džini sųt věčno goręći duhi. S jih råstom oni avtomatično učęt sę novym zaklinańjam
i čarujųt jih, črpajųći silų iz svojego ognja.
%%%%
Draconian

Drakonianci sųt universaĺni hibridi. Oni dosęgajųt zrělosti na 7 rangu i dobyvajųt
svoj kolor.
%%%%
Formicid

Formicidi sųt podobne člověku mråvji, ktori umětno kopajųt zemjų. Jih rųky i nogy
sųt izključiteljno siĺne, i oni živųt v stańju stalogo stazisa.
%%%%
Felid

Felidi sųt råzumni koti s několikymi žit́jami. Oni ne mogųt koristati bronjų ili
orųžje.
%%%%
Gale Centaur

Mogųći hibridi konjev, čiji prědki proizhodęt od samyh četyrěh větrov, Kentavri Větra
sųt najsiĺni, kȯgda bystro galopujųt do svojih neprijateljev.
%%%%
Gargoyle

Stvorjeni iz živogo kamenja, Gargulji imajųt nadprirodnų sposobnost́ k magiji zemje. U njih
jest nizko zdråvje, ale jih kamenne tělesa sųt nevěrojętno tųge.
%%%%
Gnoll

Gnolls are canine-like humanoids. They are unable to specialise in any single
skill, but instead gradually become proficient in all skills at once.
%%%%
Human

Humans are natural explorers. Discovering new places refreshes and invigorates them.
They are otherwise completely average.
%%%%
Tengu

Tengu are bird-people who love to fight, both with weapons and spells. They are
fragile but agile, and eventually learn to fly.
%%%%
Kobold

Kobolds are attuned to the shadows and can only see or be seen at a close range.
They are small and a little fragile, and are talented in most skills.
%%%%
Merfolk

Half fish, half human, Merfolk are citizens of both water and land. They are
strong combatants, and adept at using magic to assist in battle.
%%%%
Minotaur

Minotaurs are large muscular humans with bovine heads. They excel at all forms
of close and ranged combat.
%%%%
Mountain Dwarf

Mountain Dwarves are strong melee fighters and capable heavy-armoured casters.
They can use scrolls to enchant even some artefacts.
%%%%
Mummy

Compelled to walk by an ancient curse, Mummies have innate faith and are adept
at Necromancy, but learn other skills very slowly. They cannot drink potions.
%%%%
Naga

Nagas are hybrids: human from the waist up, with a snake-like tail instead of
legs. They move very slowly but are naturally resilient.
%%%%
Octopode

Octopodes are a species of amphibious cephalopods. They can wear eight rings,
but almost no armour fits them.
%%%%
Oni

Large, robust, and extremely fond of drinking, Oni make capable brawlers and
spellcasters and gain extra benefits from healing potions.
%%%%
Poltergeist

Formless ghosts who delight in hexing their enemies. They can equip many
different hats, gloves, boots, or cloaks at once, but cannot wear body armour.
%%%%
Revenant

Revenants are skeletal undead who are strong at melee combat and can empower
damage-dealing spells by consuming memories they harvest from their victims.
%%%%
Spriggan

Very small, very frail and very fast, Spriggans are highly competent stabbers
and spellcasters.
%%%%
Troll

Trolls are monstrous creatures with powerful claws. They regenerate health
rapidly.
%%%%
Vine Stalker

Frail symbionts, Vine Stalkers cannot regain health from potions. Their magical
reserves absorb damage, and they pack a magic-restoring bite.
%%%%
