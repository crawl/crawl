Throwing

Trenovańje Metańja bųde sdělati metajemo orųžje (kako napriklad kopja) vyše
akuratnym i škodlivym. Takože ono bųde povysiti pravdopodobnost́ togo, že magične tŕny
bųdųt imati poslědstva.

Armour

Uměńje Brȯnje množi bonus dlja AC, dobyvajemy pri nošeńju brȯnje vsih tipov,
vključajųći tělesnų brȯnjų, plašče, rųkavice i tako dalje. Ono takože nemnogo
smėnšaje kaznj dlja bystrosti čarovańja zaklinanij i daljnjego orųžja, objavjajųćų sę
pri nošeńju tęžkoj brȯnje.
%%%%
Dodging

Uměńje Izběgańja bųde vlivati na tvoj šans izběgti atakų bližnjų, daljnjų ili
magičnų. jednako, něktore tipy ataky nemožno izběgti, take kako čarovańja (ktorym
trěba odpirati sę silojų volje) i eksplozije.
%%%%
Stealth

Trenujųći Skrytost́, ty možeš umėnšiti pravdopodobnost́ togo, že monstri bųdųt
uględěti tebe, jestli oni spęt ili ne trėvožni, i povysiti tvoje šansy na to,
že monstri bųdųt utratiti tebe iz vida, kȯgda ty bųdeš izȯjdti iz jih polja zrěńja.
Zauvaži, že vyše moćni monstri, kako pravilo, sųt vyše podzirlivi i čutlivi.

Uměńje Skrytosti, zajedno s uměńjem orųžja, takože vlivaje na pravdopodobnost́
pričinjeńja značnoj škody pri atakovańju odvlěčenogo ili bezpomoćnogo stvora,
a takože na kolikost́ dodatnoj pričinjajemoj škody.

k izběgańju, bystrosti ataky i uspěhu čarovańja zaklinańja.

%%%%
Conjurations

Conjurations are battle oriented spells, which often work by conjuring up a
chunk of short-lived matter and propelling it towards the victim to deal direct
damage. Being skilled in Conjurations usually increases the damage and accuracy
of such spells, and sometimes affects their range or the size of the area
affected.

Being skilled in Conjurations increases the success rate and spell power of
spells in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools,
in which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill
in these schools.
%%%%
Hexes

Hexes try to bestow various enchantments on nearby things. While none do direct
damage, being ensorcelled can effectively take an opponent out of combat,
although they often require overcoming an opponent's willpower to have an effect.
Skill in Hexes can help overcome this.

Being skilled in Hexes increases the success rate and spell power of spells in
this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools, in which
case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill in these
schools.
%%%%
Summonings

Summonings deals with summoning creatures from another plane of reality to
temporarily aid you. Increasing Summonings skill may grant a number of effects
depending on the spell, such as longer duration or more powerful allies. Note
that summoned beings do not leave corpses and give no experience when killed,
and their equipment is as ephemeral as they are.

Being skilled in Summonings increases the success rate and spell power of
spells in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools,
in which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill
in these schools.
%%%%
Forgecraft

Forgecraft is the art of shaping tangible objects out of magic. Forgecraft
spells can construct a wide array of mechanical creatures that will fight
alongside the caster, as well as create traps and barricades and even turn the
physical resilience of one's armour into a weapon.

Being skilled in Forgecraft increases the success rate and spell power of
spells in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools,
in which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill
in these schools.
%%%%
Necromancy

Necromancy is the art of draining and gaining life, although most often
associated with zombies and other undead. As such, Necromancy spells have a
wide range of effects, ranging from spells to create or disrupt the undead to
spells granting unholy healing in various forms.

Being skilled in Necromancy increases the success rate and spell power of
spells in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools,
in which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill
in these schools.
%%%%
Translocations

Translocations focus on the spatial movement of beings or objects, including
useful spells to translocate yourself over short distances quickly or to
transport your enemies away from you.

Being skilled in Translocations increases the success rate and spell power of
spells in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools,
in which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill
in these schools.
%%%%
Alchemy

Alchemy is the art of toxins and transmutation. Spells in this school can twist
objects and creatures into different shapes, unleash powerful poisons, or even
unweave the fabric of magic itself.

Being skilled in Alchemy increases the success rate and spell power of
spells in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools,
in which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill
in these schools.
%%%%
Fire Magic

Fire Magic focuses mainly on dealing fire damage over long distances, with many
effective Conjurations.

Being skilled in Fire Magic increases the success rate and spell power of
spells in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools,
in which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill
in these schools.
%%%%
Ice Magic

Ice Magic contains a range of direct damage spells, as well as some defensive
effects - for example, crafting temporary ice armour.

Being skilled in Ice Magic increases the success rate and spell power of spells
in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools, in
which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill in
these schools.
%%%%
Air Magic

Air Magic provides spells to deal damage directly with the wind, as well as
noisy but powerful lightning Conjurations. It also provides some utility
spells, such as a call to the wind to assist your movement or a Hex to prevent
most use of magic in the area surrounding you.

Being skilled in Air Magic increases the success rate and spell power of spells
in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools, in
which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill in
these schools.
%%%%
Earth Magic

Earth Magic contains spells able to deal large amounts of direct physical
damage, which cannot be resisted like fire, cold and lightning damage can. It
is also linked with Alchemy, with spells to liquefy the earth around you, or to
turn your enemies to stone.

Being skilled in Earth Magic increases the success rate and spell power of
spells in this school. Many spells belong to a combination of several schools,
in which case spellcasting success and power will depend on the average skill
in these schools.

Evocations

Evocations skill affects your ability to use magical items like wands and other
uncommon objects, increasing the power or success rate of such items.
%%%%
Shapeshifting

Shapeshifting enables the use of magical talismans to change oneself into other
forms. Sufficient skill allows one to use more powerful forms without gravely
damaging one's health. Being skilled in Shapeshifting also makes most forms
somewhat stronger, in ways which vary between forms.
