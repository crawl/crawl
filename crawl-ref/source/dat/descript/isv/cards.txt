# Escape
Velocity card

Na kartě jest izobražena pusta, vetha dvojka obuvij. Jih podȯšvy sųt pokryte
pěskom, pepelom i drnom.

Iztęgańje tutoj karty može darovati velikų bystrost́ sȯjųznikam ili odstrašajųćų
pomalost́ neprijateljam.
%%%%
the Tomb card

Na kartě jest izobražena kråljevska kripta, vhod ktoroj jest zapečatany tęžkojų
kamennojų dȯskojų.

Iztęgańje tutoj karty pogrebaje jų koristnika v zaščitne, dočasne stěny iz tvŕdoj
skaly. Na nizkyh rangah moći ili iz-za sųsědnyh stvorov kripta može byti
nesȯvŕšenojų.

Kripta poddŕživaje sę blågodarę prisųtstvu jej tvorca v njej, vśako dvigańje
iz centra kripty bųde privoditi k råzdrobjeńju jej stěn.
%%%%
Exile card

Na kartě sųt izobražene mnogočisĺne rųky, izhodęće iz zějajųćego portala i
odolěvajųće panikujųćų figurų.

Iztęgańje tutoj karty može izgnati bližnjih stvorov v Bezdnų, i čim vyše rang moći,
tym vyše stvorov dostupno dlja izgnańja. Tuta karta nikȯgda ne bųde izganjati jų
koristnika.
%%%%
the Shaft card

Na kartě jest izobraženy pųtnik, padajųći v propast́, ktora otvorila sę pod jego
nogami.

Iztęgańje tutoj karty bųde stvoriti propast́ pod koristnikom i, možlivo, pod
jego neprijateljami.
%%%%
the Elixir card

Na kartě jest izobražena samodovoljny usměh, stvorjeny iz devęti čaš.

Iztęgańje tutoj karty bystro lěči zdråvje i magijų koristnika.
%%%%
the Cloud card

Na kartě sųt izobražene želězne vråta, objęte mglojų.

Iztęgańje tutoj karty obimaje neprijateljev krųgami škodlivyh oblåkov. Efekty
takyh oblåkov bųdųt měnjati sę v zavisnosti od moći kart.
%%%%
# Destruction
Vitriol card

Na kartě jest izobražena råstlina oklob, kapajųće svoj samobytny råzjedajųći sok.

Iztęgańje tutoj karty bųde aktivovati zaklinańje råzjedajųćej ataky.
%%%%
the Storm card

Na kartě jest izobraženy tonųći v morju korab v čas gromovoj burje.

Iztęgańje tutoj karty bųde aktivovati burjų větra i električne iskry. Ulěpšeńje
moći karty bųde privesti k usiljeńju sily větra i k siĺnějšim, mnogočislnějšim
iskram.
%%%%
Pain card

Na kartě jest izobraženo želězno kolo, grozęće bodcami i zarđavěvše od krvi.

Iztęgańje tutoj karty bųde aktivovati nekromantično zaklinańje ataky.
%%%%
the Orb card

The card depicts a figure surrounded by floating, glowing orbs of energy.

Drawing this card unleashes one or possibly more destructive orbs of pure magic.
%%%%
Degeneration card

The card depicts a dragon whose scales are sloughing off, revealing a sheep
underneath.

Drawing this card will attempt to polymorph and malmutate nearby enemies into
weaker forms, and will briefly daze those that cannot be polymorphed.
%%%%
Wild Magic card

The card depicts a pointy wizard's hat, scorched and resting on a pile of ashes.

Drawing this card causes wild magic (as if from miscasting a spell) to act upon
its user's enemies. Each creature affected will restore some of the user's
magic.
# Summoning
%%%%
the Elements card

The card depicts the figures of great beasts lurking in a sandstorm.

Drawing this card will summon three beasts of the four elements.
%%%%
the Pentagram card

The card depicts cultists marking out a ritual pentagram on the floor.

Drawing this card will summon a pair of demons to the user's aid. There is a
small chance one of the demons will be hostile.
%%%%
the Dance card

The card depicts an extravagantly-dressed, masked figure dancing alone in a
vast room.

Drawing this card will summon a dancing weapon. Its quality, brand, and
friendliness are all affected by card power.
%%%%
the Swarm card

The card depicts a bear surrounded by a swarm of bees. It hums ominously.

Drawing this card will summon bees.
%%%%
the Rangers card

The card depicts an arrow in a bull's-eye, which has been split by a second
arrow.

Drawing this card will summon several archers.
%%%%
the Illusion card

The card depicts someone standing in a hall of mirrors, surrounded by their own
reflections.

Drawing this card will summon an illusion of the user. Its power will depend
upon one's own.
%%%%
# Punishment
Wrath card

The card depicts the hand of the divine, reaching down from the heavens to hurl
furious lightning.

Drawing this card will draw a god's wrath against the user.
%%%%
the Wraith card

The card depicts absolutely nothing.

Drawing this card will drain the user.
%%%%
the Swine card

The card depicts several feral hogs wallowing in a mud pit.

Drawing this card will make its user turn pink and root for truffles.
%%%%
Torment card

The card depicts a screaming figure being tortured on the rack.

Drawing this card will cause agonising injury to the user.
